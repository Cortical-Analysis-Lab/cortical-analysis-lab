Dr. Shaun James

Principal Investigator, Cortical Analysis Lab • Assistant Professor of Neuroscience

Biography

Dr. Shaun James is an Assistant Professor of Neuroscience at Sacred Heart University and Principal Investigator of the Cortical Analysis Lab. His research integrates multimodal neuroscience methods—including wide-field optical imaging, electrophysiology (EEG), and behavioral analysis—to investigate cortical network function and dysfunction.
