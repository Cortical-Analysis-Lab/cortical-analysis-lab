Cortical Analysis Lab

Exploring the interplay between cortical dynamics, neurovascular coupling, and behavior through multimodal imaging, EEG, and computational analysis.

Our Focus

The Cortical Analysis Lab uses a tripartite approach to to characterize the dysfunction of neurological disorders and evaluate therapeutic efficacy.

Our approach synchronizes optical imaging, EEG, and behavioral assessment to investigate the global and regional changes in the cortical function of clinically relevant rodent models of mice and rats.
