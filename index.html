Our Research Focus

The Cortical Analysis Lab investigates the interplay between neural, vascular, and metabolic systems to better understand brain function and dysfunction. We combine optical imaging, EEG, and behavioral analysis to map how the brain coordinates activity during cognitive and physiological states.
