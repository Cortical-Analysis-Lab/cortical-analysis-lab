Cortical Analysis Lab

Exploring the interplay between cortical dynamics, neurovascular coupling, and behavior through multimodal imaging, EEG, and computational analysis.

Our Focus

We investigate how neural, glial, and vascular components interact during complex behavioral states, using synchronized optical imaging, EEG, and behavioral tracking systems.

Recent Highlights

New multimodal EEG–optical imaging integration allows real-time mapping of cortical activation during behavior. Our latest preprints explore temporal correlations between hemodynamic and electrophysiological signatures in anesthetized mice.
